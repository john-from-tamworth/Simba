BARBERING ACADEMY

Our Courses

Level 1 - Total Beginner

Never picked up a pair of clippers in your life? Thinking of starting a career in barbering or just want to save some money and want to learn how to cut your kids hair? This is the course for you! This course you will learn how to cut a basic short back and sides using clipper and scissors.

Level 2 - Hair dresser to Barber

Are you at college studying hair? Are you a hairdresser that wants to go into barbering? Level 2 is perfect to brush up on your barbering skills. Learn how to cut a mans hair to a barber standard. We make fine art out of blending, fading and tapering specifically during this course.

Level 3 Skin fade

Barber alert!! Do you want to brush up, gain confidence or enhance in your skinfade technique? If your a barber or hairdresser that is learning on the job you may often find your employer doesn't have time to give you the hands on practical work you need..its hard right when they are running a busy shop! Sign up! Employers get 15% discount on group sessions and we will come to you!!

Level 4 Afro Barber Tech

Don't stop there!! A master barber should be able to do any type of hair including afro! Enhance your skills and learn how to cut and care for afro hair using various cutting tools and techniques
